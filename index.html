Lessons Learned Thus Far

How far have I come?

I have to say that when I started taking this course I did not know what to expect. Compared to what I did in my previous life chapter, learning how to write HTML was what one my call a "gamble". But so far I have figued out how to effectivly use the tools and resources that have been provided. To my suprise, I have come to enjoy writing these web pages and look forward to learning more indepth material. Below are some learned techniques I apply before a "study sesson" as well as a few websites I find very useful.

Studying 101: Study Smarter Not Harder

  1. Exercise before I sit down at my machine.
  2. Ensure I have some classical music to jam out to.
  3. Set scheduled breaks every hour regardless of how much i am in the zone.

Studying 101: Study Smarter Not Harder is a handout produced by the Learning Center, University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.
